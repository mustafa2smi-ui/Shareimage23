Q: Namaz ke waqt me mobile use karna kaisa hai?

A: Namaz ke dauran kisi bhi duniawi amal me lagna, jaise mobile chalana, namaz tod deta hai. Sirf zarurat ke waqt (jaise Quran app ya azaan check) me ijtinaab behtar hai.

Reference: Fatawa Alamgiri, Vol. 1, Page 234
Answered by: Mufti Saeed Ahmed
